--- title: Mama Ines address: Calle Hortaleza 22 layout: madrid tag: madrid-chueca ---

Just about the only decent cafe around here open at this time on a Sunday, this is actually quite a good place. It has a pleasant atmosphere, with cool lighting and paintings, and comfortable furniture. Unsurprisingly, given the Chueca location, this is a gay cafe, but only in a fairly low-key way; the only uncomfortable thing is the ghastly pale-green daubed and reddish-purple walls.
