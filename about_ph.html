Who I am

I was a serial expat, because it’s easier to fit in when you're supposed not to, but I started living and working in country number six in 2000 and didn’t leave. Not yet, anyway.

In the photo (right), I am fourth from left. This was taken in Libeek, the Netherlands, in August 1999. From the left to right, the people in the photo are: Marc Smits, Szilvia Biro, Russell, Peter Hilton, Alex Zeffertt, Carl Dolby, Marion Smits, Fleur Van Rootselaar and Jean-Didier Garaud.

Where I am now

  • Working (as software developer) for Lunatech Research in Rotterdam, the Netherlands. You can contact me by sending e-mail to or by ringing +31 10 7502600 during office hours, Dutch time. I don’t just work, of course, and if I’m in a cafe somewhere then I won’t answer the phone.
  • Singing (baritone) with Cappella Gabrieli, a Rotterdam chamber choir.
  • Living (like a king) in central Rotterdam with my partner, Marion.

Where I’ve been (the short version)

  • Working for Logica, which later became LogicaCMG, in Rotterdam (2000-2004).
  • Eight months working at SmithKline Beecham in Surrey (1999).
  • Three and a half weeks working at the ODCCP in Vienna (1999).
  • Seven months working at SES-Astra in Luxembourg (1998-1999).
  • One year working at CSC Computer Sciences Ltd in Cambridge (1997-1998).
  • Seven months working at EUTELSAT in Paris (1996-1997).
  • Three years studying pure mathematics at the University Of Bristol (1992-1994, 1995-1996).
  • Ten months as an Erasmus student at l’Université de Bordeaux I and studying French stuff at the Maison du DEFLE (1994-1995).
  • Lots of Monday nights working at Avon Phototypesetting Ltd (1994).
  • Four or five terms working on Epigram, the University of Bristol Independent Students’ Newspaper, back in the days before newspapers had web sites (1993-1995).
  • A long summer holiday working as a deck-hand on board the Aran Flyer, helping tourists in Ireland across Galway Bay to the Aran Islands.
  • My first year of university living in Churchill Hall, University of Bristol (1992).
  • Six years going to school at Boundstone Community College (1986-1992).
  • Four years at Thornberry Middle School and a few years at what was then South Lancing Primary School (1979-1986).
  • Moved from Kent to South Lancing, West Sussex, UK (1977).
