Recommendation: try it out if you are in the area.

Hokkaido is an unlikely find: decent competition to Dim Daily outside the town centre - a small Japanese/Chinese noodle bar, with pretty decent udon soup or stir-fry, as well as the obligatory sushi.

Unfortunately, the atmosphere is half take-away, with a television on the wall.
